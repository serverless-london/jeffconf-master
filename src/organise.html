Organise a JeffConf/JessConf/JexxConf/{insert name here}?

An epithet fluid series of community organised conferences focusing on the future of software design in a cloud native serverless world.

They are not for profit, locally organised conferences with the aim of sharing knowledge and increasing diversity within the technology community.

Anyone can organise one, as long as you stick to the guiding principles:-

    Diversity is prioritised
    Not run for profit
    Ticket prices are kept low to make it accessible to the local community
    Have fun doing it
